Course 01 — The Read

Where the kitchen stands today

Zoila's Kitchen already has the hardest part — a genuine, recipe-driven story, real reviews, and four real service lines. The opportunity is making that story impossible to miss in Welland & Niagara search and social feeds.

What's already working

  • A warm, founder-led brand story — Chef Zoila's voice comes through on every page, which builds trust faster than any stock-photo competitor.
  • Four distinct offers already exist: weekly meal prep, student packages, senior meal solutions, and intimate event catering — a full menu of price points and audiences.
  • A live, modern website with a working quote-request path and a testimonials page — the bones of a lead-gen engine are already in place.

Where the opportunity sits

  • No visible Google Business Profile presence in "meal prep near me" or "catering Welland" searches — the single highest-intent local search moment.
  • Social channels are underused as a discovery engine — food is inherently visual, and there's no consistent posting or short-video system converting scrollers into inquiries.
  • No structured way to turn a one-time catering guest into a recurring weekly meal-prep subscriber — the highest-value customer type is being left on the table.
Course 02 — Your Guests

Who's actually hungry

Every course on this menu is built around four real Niagara-region guests — not generic personas.

Weekly Regular

The Busy Household

Dual-income Welland & Niagara families who want home-cooked dinners without the Sunday prep marathon. Books weekly, values consistency.

Campus Crowd

The Niagara College Student

Time-poor, budget-conscious, discovered almost entirely through Instagram/TikTok — needs affordable, filling, easy-to-order packages.

Caring Adult Child

The Senior's Family

Often books on behalf of a parent. Searches Google, reads reviews carefully, and values a phone number that's easy to find and a real human to talk to.

Occasion Planner

The Event Host

Planning a shower, milestone birthday, or family gathering. Browses the catering menu in detail and compares 2–3 caterers before requesting a quote.

Course 03 — The Menu

Six courses of strategy

Each course is a self-contained channel with its own tactics — served in sequence, but built to compound.

01Amuse-Bouche
Local Search & Google Business Profile

Get found first, before the recipe even matters

"Meal prep near me" and "catering Welland" are the two highest-intent phrases in this business — right now Zoila's Kitchen is nearly invisible for both. This course claims the map pack.

Google Business Profile build-out Service-area & category optimization Weekly photo & post cadence On-site local SEO (Welland, Niagara, St. Catharines)
02First Course
Website Conversion Redesign

Turn browsers into booked quotes

The site's story is strong — the job is shortening the distance between "this looks delicious" and a submitted quote request, on mobile especially.

Sticky "Get a Quote" flow Menu-forward mobile layout Page speed & image optimization Testimonials woven into every service page
03Second Course
Content & Social Media Engine

Let the food do the talking

A consistent kitchen-to-camera content system — prep footage, plating, family stories — built to travel on Reels and get shared inside Welland community groups.

Weekly short-video content calendar Behind-the-kitchen Instagram/Facebook system Niagara community group & page seeding UGC & guest photo repurposing
04Main Course
Paid Lead Generation

Put the menu in front of the right table

Targeted Meta & Google campaigns split by guest type — weekly meal prep for families, promotional packages for students, and quote-request ads for event hosts.

Meta lead-form ads (families & students) Google Search ads (catering & senior meals) Geo-targeting: Welland, Niagara Falls, St. Catharines, Port Colborne Retargeting website visitors
05Cheese Course
Email & SMS Nurture

Turn a single order into a standing one

A quiet automation layer that reminds weekly meal-prep guests it's ordering day, welcomes new subscribers, and win-backs anyone who's gone a month without ordering.

Welcome & first-order sequence Weekly "menu's up" SMS reminder 30-day win-back flow Post-catering-event follow-up
06Dessert
Reviews, Referrals & Community Partnerships

Let happy guests set the table for the next one

A structured review-request system, a simple refer-a-friend credit, and light partnerships with senior centres, Niagara College groups, and local offices.

Automated Google review requests Refer-a-friend meal credit Senior centre & campus partnerships Corporate lunch/office catering outreach
Course 04 — Service Flow

From stranger to regular

Every course on the menu feeds one continuous path — the way a guest actually moves from first glance to standing weekly order.

Discover

Found via Google Maps, local SEO, or a shared Reel.

Crave

Engages with kitchen content, saves a post, visits the site.

Order

Submits a quote request or first meal-prep order.

Return

Nurtured by email/SMS into a weekly standing order.

Refer

Leaves a review, refers a friend, books the next event.
